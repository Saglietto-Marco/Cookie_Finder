Mission Statement

"We aim to help small entrepreneurs setup an online store front to reach more customers."

As we continue to make advancements technologically, we find ourselves becoming more reliant on the internet. We trend towards using services that'll make things more convenient to discover locations near by or at a particular location. Currently there are no such services for young entrepreneurs to help them grow their business reaching new potential customres on the online front. We want to help facilitate such an environment where our clients can have a store front online and inform potential customers where and when they will be at a particular location.
